The Living Killing Life



Video

Early version year 2016 in the "Global Green USA Pre Oscar Party".
"The Living Killing Life" is a preview of the future album that Stevie Wonder will make. The first time he presented the song in public was during the "Global Green USA Pre Oscar Party in 2016". It seems that she composed it in 2010 after the earthquake in Haiti. This new song was again performed by Stevie -already with full instrumentation- on September 25 at The Greek Theatre in Los Angeles, during his presence at Global Citizen Live this year 2021. The releasing this will be as a charity single to help the United Nations.
