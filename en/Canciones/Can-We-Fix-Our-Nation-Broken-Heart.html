Can We Fix Our Nation’s Broken Heart


Composer music * Stevie Wonder
Composer lyrics * Stevie Wonder and Mauli Bonner
Produced and arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Kay-Ta: Acustic guitar; Stevie Wonder: Piano, Bass, Percussion, Harmonica...

Video

"Can We Fix Our Nation’s Broken Heart" a new song that was released on August 30, 2024. A call for unity and to heal the divisions of a country through the clean, acoustic sound of guitars and percussion, with lyrics that are socially committed to the fight for understanding. "Can we fix our nation's broken heart? Are we brave enough to try?" Reflects about a nation that finds itself mired in chaos, misunderstanding and pain.

This release comes shortly after his appearance at the Democratic National Convention in Chicago in support of Kamala Harris. There he encouraged everyone present to get more actively involved and take advantage of the historic moment that the country and the world are experiencing. In his speech, phrases to keep in mind such as: "And now is the time to understand where we are and what it will take to win — win the broken hearts, win the disenchanted, win the angry spirits. Now is the time. This is the moment to remember, when you tell your children where you were and what you did. As we stand between history’s pain and tomorrow’s promises, we must choose courage over complacency. It is time to get up and go vote!" Later performed "Higher Ground" reminding everyone of the need to rise above divisions and work towards a much brighter future.
