Love's In Need of Stevie Today;
The Universe Is Watching Us

New times are running for Stevie Wonder in this 2020. It seems that he has overcome his health problems with the kidney transplant to which he was subjected, he appears relaxed with his children Kailand and Mandla Morris on the cover of Odda magazine -biannual culture magazine and contemporary fashion- of the month of September 2020. He can also be seen active and excited in communications calling for a social change. "The Universe Is Watching Us" Stevie Wonder tells us, comparing life to a song that's out of sync, wondering if we can feel how life is out of tune.

On the other hand, his musical career is rewarded once again with the appearance of several of his albums among the list of the 500 best albums of all time that The Rolling Stone magazine has updated this September 2020. "Songs In The Key Of Life" ranks a creditable fourth on a list that tops another Motown great, Marvin Gaye.

The world is in turmoil, bleeding from instability and social inequalities, from a global pandemic that is devouring the life to which we have settled. We need to change, improve our attitude towards nature to prevent us from reaching an irreversible collapse. As you could say using one of his emblematic songs, Love's In Need of Stevie Today, and luckily Stevie Wonder is recovered when we need him most, but we all have to do our part so that this life is not out of tune.
