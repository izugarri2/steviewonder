SCANDAL: Future Sunny Days

2018
Future Sunny Days is a song composed by Stevie Wonder exclusively for the seventh and final season of the acclaimed and successful television series Scandal, broadcast on April 19 of this year 2018.

Shonda Rhimes -producer and creator of the series- had already used some others Stevie Wonder theme to musically embellish the series, since it is one of her favorite artists, but it was quite a surprise the inclusion of this theme almost at the last moment.
